# Risk Of Ramen 
A modded expansion for Risk of Rain 2! Contains a few items with more content to come. 
If you have any suggestions, ideas, or bugs to report, feel free to DM me on Discord at ramenguy221!

## ITEMS
- Denku Rope Coil (White)
  - Critical strikes deal 10% (+10% per stack) more damage.
- Obsidian Card (Green)
  - Your barrier decreases 25% (+10% per stack) slower.
- Wax Idol (Green)
  - Gain 50 (+10 per stack) armor when you're ignited. Summon a friendly Wisp minion (currently, the minion doesn't work.)
- Stained Belt (Green)
  - Gain critical strike chance equal to 25% (+25% per stack) of your barrier.
